Meet Me

Rosin Mare is the pen name I chose not to hide behind, but to write through—a voice for the parts of me silence couldn't hold. Writing is my solitude, my rebellion, and my survival. Inspired by Kafka, I don’t write to showcase pain, but to survive it. The Rose Garden is my refuge, where beauty and truth can grow wild. I write for the raw, the restless, the reflective—for those who feel too much and say too little. If you find yourself here, you were always meant to.
